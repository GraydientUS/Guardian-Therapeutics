    Hematology

    We have partnered the product rights related to our VWF aptamer program with Suzhou Ribo Life Sciences Ltd. within Greater China, while retaining global ex-China product rights for ourselves.

    Our Clinical stage VWF aptamer product candidate is being developed in partnership with the Medical University of Vienna as a pro-hemostatic gent for treatment of hereditary bleeding disorders.

    Additional Discovery stage VWF aptamer drug candidates to be developed as anti-thrombotic agents for prevention of stroke in high risk populations are available for partnering.
